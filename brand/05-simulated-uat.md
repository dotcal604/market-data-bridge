## Cohort 3: Design-Sensitive Reviewers (brand designers, creative directors)

**Simulated participants: 3 designers who judge marks on craft, originality, and durability.**

### Direction 1: The Join

| Criterion | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| 3-second impression | "Convergence diagram. More illustration than mark." |
| What they think it does | "Data flow visualization tool." |

**Biggest risk for Direction 1**: Too many parts, collapses at small sizes, feels like an illustration rather than a mark.

**Biggest risk for Direction 2**: Represents eval engine, not bridge. Needs reframing to work as whole-product identity.

**Path forward**: Iterate both, then hybridize — use Direction 2's grid structure but reframe the semantic meaning toward "bridge" (three source columns → unified output row). Keep Direction 1's narrative clarity for banners and extended brand applications.

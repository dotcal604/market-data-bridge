# Holly Autopsy (`/holly/autopsy`)

Deep analysis of Holly AI historical trades. Shows where profits come from and where they leak.

## Strategy Leaderboard

Strategies ranked by total P&L, showing:
- Win rate, average R, Sharpe ratio
- Profit factor (gross wins / gross losses)
- Average giveback ratio
- Trade count

## MFE/MAE Profiles

For each strategy, shows:
- **MFE** (Maximum Favorable Excursion) — how far price moved in your favor
- **MAE** (Maximum Adverse Excursion) — how far price moved against you
- **Giveback ratio** — what % of MFE you returned before exiting

## Segment Comparison

Side-by-side cards comparing Holly Grail vs Holly Neo (and other segments) on P&L, win rate, average profit, and hold time.

## Holly Performance (`/holly/performance`)

Trailing stop optimization results:
- 19 trailing stop strategies simulated against historical trades
- Per-strategy best exit method
- P&L improvement metrics

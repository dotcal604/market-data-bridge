# Market Data (`/market`)

## Quote Lookup
Enter a symbol to get real-time quote (IBKR when connected, Yahoo fallback). Shows bid, ask, last, OHLC, volume, and change %.

## Options (`/market/options`)
Full options chain for any symbol:
- Select expiration date
- View calls and puts with bid/ask, IV, open interest, volume
- Greeks when available

## Financials (`/market/financials`)
Company fundamentals:
- Revenue, margins, debt ratios
- Analyst targets and recommendations
- Earnings history (actual vs estimate)
- Sector and industry classification

# Account (`/account`)

## Account Summary
Net liquidation value, cash balance, buying power, and margin usage from IBKR.

## Portfolio Exposure
- **Gross/net exposure** — total long + short market value
- **% deployed** — how much of your capital is in positions
- **Beta-weighted exposure** — portfolio beta vs SPY
- **Portfolio heat** — aggregate risk metric
- **Sector breakdown** — exposure by sector

## Flatten Controls
Emergency flatten button that closes all positions at market and cancels all open orders. Also shows EOD auto-flatten configuration.

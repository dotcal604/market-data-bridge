# Journal (`/journal`)

Manual trade journal for recording reasoning and learning from outcomes.

## Journal Entries
Each entry includes:
- **Symbol** and trade details
- **Reasoning** — why you took the trade
- **Setup type** — breakout, pullback, reversal, gap fill, momentum
- **Confidence** — 1 (low), 2 (medium), 3 (high)
- **Tags** — categorization labels
- **Rule followed** — did you follow your own rules?
- **Outcome tags** — post-trade classification
- **Notes** — post-trade reflection

## Timeline (`/journal/timeline`)
Chronological view of all journal entries with date grouping.

## New Entry (`/journal/new`)
Form for creating a new journal entry with all fields above.
